An x-ray reveals that Homer has a crayon lodged in his brain that has been there since he was six years old. Upon removal of the crayon, Homer's IQ suddenly jumps to a very high level; he and Lisa form a great new bond, but when his way of life changes drastically he wonders if removing the crayon was a good idea.
