Opening - Mr. Burns scares the hell out of the Simpsons on Halloween.
 Hex and the City - A segment that tells the story of how the Simpsons deal with a Gypsy's curse.
 House of Whacks - The Simpsons buy a computerized upgrade for their house, and the house develops an obsessive attraction to Marge.
 Wiz Kids - A Harry Potter parody about student wizards Bart and Lisa and an evil plot of the dark Lord Montymort and his snake Slithers.
