Marge mistakenly receives breast implants, to her dismay (but to Homer's delight), and embarks on a new career as a trade show model. Meanwhile, a stunt by Bart results in a a big public image problem for Krusty, and Bart hatches a plan to help him. Bart's plan goes awry, but Marge's enhanced bosoms end up saving the day when she reveals her boobs and goes topless in public.
