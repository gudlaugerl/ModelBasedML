When Bart discovers that a fifth grader has a scar identical to one Bart has, Marge tells him about a club of four mothers she was in seven years earlier; when Marge plans to reunite with the mothers, while Homer reunites with the fathers and Bart with the boys, matters turn out much better for Marge than for Homer or Bart, who, after hearing the story from Jeffery Albertson about how they got their scars, tries to break the group up.
