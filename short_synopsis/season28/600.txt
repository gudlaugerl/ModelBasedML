The episode has a Hunger Games theme. The three main segments include:
 Dry Hard - Mr. Burns introduces a Hunger Games-style contest in which Springfield's children fight each other for a day in Burns' personal reservoir.
 BFF R.I P. - Lisa's imaginary best friend, Rachel, is jealous of Lisa's real best friends and starts killing them so she could spend more time with her.
 Moefinger - Moe tells Bart that the barflies are actually covert agents and that he wants Bart to join their team in Homer's place.
