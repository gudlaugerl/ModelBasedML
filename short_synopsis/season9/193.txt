After finally accepting that his comedic style is outdated, Krusty settles on edgier material: what's wrong with today's crop of stand-up comedians. Guest starring Jay Leno, Steven Wright, Janeane Garofalo, Bobcat Goldthwait and Bruce Baum, and featuring Hank Williams, Jr. The name of this episode is very similar to an earlier episode "The Last Temptation of Homer."
